What the honors mean

AP Scholar with Distinction

Average of 3.5 or higher on all exams, with scores of 3 or higher on five or more exams.

AP Scholar with Honor

Average of 3.25 or higher on all exams, with scores of 3 or higher on four or more exams.

AP Scholar

Scores of 3 or higher on three or more AP exams.

Each scholar's card lists the exams they passed, meaning a score of 3 or higher. The number on each chip is the score, colored by value: 5 deep navy, 4 sky, 3 steel. Scores below 3 are left off. Names shown are the recognized scholars. AP IDs, student IDs, and dates of birth from the source files are left out on purpose. Tap any grade heading to collapse or expand it.
